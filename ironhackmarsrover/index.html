MARS ROVER IRON HACK

We are sending a rover to Mars, and we need to be able to move it through a series of instructions sent from the Earth. As the final exercise for this part of the module, you will be leading this project.

Enter an array of character commands for each rover, which can include:

    These commands will be the first letter of either (f)orward, (r)ight, or (l)eft. To test it, use the string: ‘rffrfflfrff’.

  • f for go forward
  • b for go back
  • r for turn right
  • l for turn left

Follow the Rover:

The rovers will be represented as their letter id and the direction (N,E,S,W) they are facing.

Obstacles will be represented as a Thunder Lightning symbol.

The robots are smart enough to detect the obstacles and other rovers in their path before landing on them. Review the "Rovers' Log" to keep track.
